A Delicious Lasagna Recipe

This certain recipe is well-receiveed worldwide, and has been ranked as one of the most popular lasagna recipes in the internet, racking up an approximate number of 7 million views every year. While this takes more than a little bit more effort to prepare, it sure is worth the time with its satisfying and filling taste!

Needed Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Step-by-Step Method

  1. Step 1: Gather all the listed ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Cook the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic together in an oven over medium heat until it looks well browned
  3. Step 3: Add in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water - then stir it all in. Then, season with sugar, 2 tablespoons of parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon of salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeda, and pepper. Simmer it while covered, then wait for 1 ½ hours while stirring occasionally.
  4. Step 4: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  5. Step 5: In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  6. Step 6: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. Step 7: To assemble, spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
